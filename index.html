Dublin Youth Innovation

A student-led nonprofit organization dedicated to providing STEM opportunities for the underserved youth.


Our Mission

Bridging the Education Gap

Underserved youth are significantly underrepresented in STEM fields, limiting their opportunities in a technology-driven world. DYI works to empower the next generation through STEM experiences.

Inspiring Future Engineers

DYI provides opportunities through in-person workshops, robot demonstrations, robot-kit drives, weekly online classes, and starting new robotics teams. By providing hands-on learning opportunities, we hope to motivate the youth to explore engineering that aren't by provided by low-funded schools and destitute communities.

Staying Connected

DYI fosters a close community by sending monthly newsletters with updates and announcements, while actively maintaining social media to keep students engaged and connected with us.

About Us

Athena Coco Hernandez

In every coding class Coco took, she was the only Hispanic student which often made her feel alienated and uncomfortable. So, Coco went on to found CCC with one goal in mind: closing the Hispanic gap within the field of computer science one kid at a time.

10+ Schools

Our students come from many different schools in both the United States and Mexico including cities like San Diego, Chula Vista, San Ysidro, Tijuana, and Mexico City.

10+ Mentors

Our tutors speak both Spanish and English. We are always looking for more to mentor due to the expanding amount of students and need for small class sizes. Our ideal ratio is 4 students per mentor.
